About Us

Join NeighbourLink in our mission to reduce carbon emissions while consistently providing excellent service to our customers.

Our Mission

At NeighbourLink, our mission is to build stronger, more connected communities by providing a reliable and convenient ride-sharing platform. We believe that transportation should be accessible, affordable, and sustainable, fostering connections and support among neighbors. As the world increasingly embraces eco-conscious and sustainable transportation solutions, our mission is to provide an innovative, user-friendly web-based car-sharing platform. Designed to meet the growing demand for environmentally-friendly mobility options, NeighbourLink goes beyond merely reducing carbon footprints; it fosters community cohesion and promotes meaningful interaction among drivers and riders.

Our Vision

Our vision for NeighbourLink is to expand our community beyond the city limits of Sydney, extending our reach across Australia, and ultimately connecting with customers in every corner of the globe. By 2030, we aspire for NeighbourLink to emerge as the foremost car-sharing company in the market, playing a pivotal role in significantly reducing carbon emissions worldwide. Through our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and community engagement, we aim to redefine the future of transportation, fostering a greener, more connected world for generations to come.

Our Values

  • Safety

    Your safety is our top priority. All our drivers undergo thorough background checks, and their vehicles are regularly inspected to ensure they meet our high standards.

  • Community

    We aim to foster a sense of community by connecting neighbors and encouraging mutual support. Our community networks and events provide opportunities for members to connect and help each other.

  • Sustainability

    We are committed to promoting sustainable transportation options. By sharing rides, we reduce our carbon footprint and contribute to a cleaner, greener environment.

  • Trust

    Trust is the foundation of our service. We strive to maintain transparency and reliability in all our interactions, ensuring that you can count on NeighbourLink for your transportation needs.
