Frequently asked questions

If you have any questions, please refer to our comprehensive FAQ section for detailed information about our services. Should you have additional inquiries, do not hesitate to contact us directly.

General

What is NeighbourLink?

At the heart of NeighborLink is a seamless and intuitive interface that connects drivers and riders within local communities. By facilitating shared rides, NeighbourLink not only aim to decrease carbon emissions but also to forge genuine connections between ‘neighbours’, transforming strangers into familiar faces along the way.

How do I register?

To register, simply click on the "Register" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details. Once registered, you'll be able to access all our services.

Is NeighbourLink available in my area?

NeighbourLink is currently available in selected regions. Please check our service areas to see if we are operating in your location.

Services

What services does NeighbourLink offer?

NeighbourLink provides ride-sharing services that allow individuals to request a ride or become a driver themselves. To join as a driver, simply register on our platform. For more detailed information, please visit our services page.

How do I book a ride with NeighbourLink?

To book a ride, simply log in to your account, go to the Ride section, and follow the prompts to schedule your ride.

How do I accept a ride with NeighbourLink?

To accept a ride, first ensure that you have registered your account as a driver with NeighbourLink. Navigate to the Driver section and follow the prompts to accept a ride.

What safety measures are in place for rides and drives?

NeighbourLink prioritizes the safety of both riders and drivers. We conduct comprehensive background checks for all users and ensure that drivers' vehicles undergo regular inspections. Additionally, we adhere to all local health guidelines to provide a safe and pleasant experience for everyone.

Payments and Fees

How do I pay for services on NeighbourLink?

Payments can be made through our secure online payment system using a credit card or through other supported payment methods listed on our payment methods page under the Profile section.

Are there any membership fees?

NeighbourLink does not charge membership fees. You only pay for the services you use. For detailed pricing, you may discuss the costs and payment methods (based on distance, time, petrol, etc.) directly with the driver. NeighbourLink is here to connect riders with drivers and support these transactions and ensure that pricing is reasonable, preventing overcharges for any services.

Can I get a refund if I cancel a service?

Refunds are available under certain conditions. For booked rides, refunds are only available if the cancellation is made at least 24 hours before the scheduled ride. For instant rides, refunds are possible only if both the driver and rider mutually agree to cancel the ride. Please review our cancellation policy for more information.

Support

How can I contact NeighbourLink support?

You can reach our support team by emailing support@NeighbourLink.co.au, calling our support hotline, or using the live chat feature on our website.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with a service?

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team immediately. We are committed to resolving any problems promptly and ensuring a positive experience for all users.

Account Management

How do I update my account information?

You can update your account information by logging into your profile on our profile page and making the necessary changes. On the profile page, you can also add any special requirements you might have (e.g., wheelchair accessibility, pet-friendly) so we can assist you in matching to an appropriate neighbour.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I delete my NeighbourLink account?

If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team with your request. They will guide you through the process and ensure your account is deleted securely.

Community and Engagement

How can I participate in NeighbourLink community networks?

To participate in NeighbourLink community networks, you can join by registering on our platform and engaging with our community activities. Visit our Community page to find local events, volunteer opportunities, and forums where you can connect with other neighbours. Stay updated with our newsletter and social media channels for the latest news and events. Your participation helps build a stronger, more connected community.

How can I provide feedback about NeighbourLink?

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback through our contact page or by emailing feedback@NeighbourLink.co.au. Your input helps us improve our services to better meet our users' needs and expectations.
